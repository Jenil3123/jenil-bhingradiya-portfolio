## Overview

Designed and developed a clean, engaging, and conversion-focused Shopify experience for Pawblaze, a pet-care brand built around comfort, care, and everyday usability for pets and their owners. The project focused on creating a friendly yet trustworthy shopping experience that clearly communicates product value, simplifies decision-making, and supports repeat purchases. Emphasis was placed on clear visual hierarchy, smooth navigation, and a scalable storefront optimized for both performance and long-term growth.

## Key Features

- **Figma Plugin Integration**: Built reusable and flexible Shopify sections for featured products, collections, brand highlights, and trust-driven content—ensuring product benefits and use cases are communicated clearly and effectively.
- **Pet-Friendly, Modern Design System**: Implemented a clean and approachable visual system using soft color tones, balanced spacing, and readable typography to align with Pawblaze’s caring, pet-centric brand identity.
- **Conversion-Optimized Product Pages**: Designed product pages with clear pricing, intuitive variant selection, concise product descriptions, key benefits, and prominent call-to-action placement to improve buyer confidence and reduce friction.
- **Mobile-First & Performance Optimized**: Optimized layouts, images, and interactions for fast load times and seamless mobile usability, ensuring a smooth shopping experience across all devices where most users browse and purchase.

## Technologies Used

- **Shopify (Custom Theme Development)**: Used to build a scalable, maintainable, and brand-aligned e-commerce storefront.
- **Liquid, HTML, CSS & JavaScript**: For developing custom sections, layouts, and lightweight interactive elements tailored to the brand’s needs.
- **Figma**: For UI planning, layout structuring, and maintaining design consistency throughout the build process.

## Challenges and Learnings

One of the key challenges was balancing a friendly, playful pet-brand aesthetic with strong product visibility and conversion clarity. This was solved by simplifying layouts, reducing unnecessary visual clutter, and prioritizing content hierarchy. Another important learning was designing for trust—ensuring product information, ease of navigation, and performance worked together to create a reliable and confidence-driven shopping experience.
## Outcome

The final result is a clean, approachable, and performance-optimized Shopify store for Pawblaze that effectively reflects the brand’s values. With improved structure, thoughtful UI decisions, and conversion-focused layouts, the website provides a strong foundation for scaling the brand’s online presence while delivering a smooth and trustworthy shopping experience across devices.
